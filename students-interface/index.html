GPA Track

Track, query, predict, and plan your grades end-to-end.
  • 1
    Import your full transcript (PDF) Drop your CUHK(SZ) transcript — we auto-extract every course, grade, and semester.
  • 2
    Visualize your grade trajectory See every letter grade, each semester's GPA, and your CGPA on one interactive chart.
  • 3
    Set a target CGPA & track distance Type your target GPA — see exactly where you stand vs. where you want to be.
  • 4
    Predict & plan future grades Add hypothetical grades for upcoming semesters — instantly see your projected CGPA.
  • 5
    Export to Excel for advisor meetings One click → a clean Excel with courses, grades, GPA, skill scores — share with your advisor.
🎯

Skills Track

Know what skills your courses build — and what's missing.
  • 1
    A course database with 308 courses Every course is scored on 9 major + 9 general skill dimensions, derived from real syllabi.
  • 2
    See your skill ability profile Each skill bar shows where you are: Exposure → Foundation → Proficiency → Mastery.
  • 3
    Spot your strengths & gaps Top-3 strongest skills surface automatically. Gaps below 25 are flagged for action.
  • 4
    Get course recommendations Pick a target skill — we rank the courses that build it most efficiently, filtered by term.
  • 5
    Compare against your category average Red marker on each bar = your category's mean. Instantly see where you out- or under-perform.
